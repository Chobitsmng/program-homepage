Hoth

Hoth is located in the outer rim and is in the Hoth System. It is a desolate, frozen tundra planet with almost no life. Tauntauns can be found here but the king of this frozen waste land is the Wampa. A winter sasquatch type beast with horns and an appetite for meat.

Hoth was a part of the Republic and after the start of the war became a rebel base for those who wanted to fight the Empire. Unfortunately, the base is found by the Empire and after a quick battle the base is lost and Hoth falls into the hands of the dark side.
